								<--  8085 Micro Processor  -->

It is a 8 bit Micro Processor introduced by intel in 1976.

--> Features of 8085:

1. 8 bit Data Bus:
	It can handle 8 bits of data simultaneously.

2. 16 bit Address Bus:
	Can address up to 64 kb of memory.

3. Clock - Speed:
	It operates at the maximum frequency of 3 MHz.

4. Power Supply:
	It requires +5 V of DC power supply.

5. Instructions Set:
	It supports 74 instructions including arithmetic, logical, control, & data transfer instructions.

6. Registers:
	It includes general purpose and special purpose registers for computation and data manipulation.
